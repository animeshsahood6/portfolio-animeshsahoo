Work / Scalable Ads

Ad systems, not one-off ads.

Scalable creative systems designed to maintain consistency across formats, platforms and audiences.

Harley-Davidson - Flex Motion System

Challenge
Develop a scalable advertising system that could adapt seamlessly across multiple display formats while maintaining Harley-Davidson’s premium visual identity.
Design Thinking
Built a modular creative framework with reusable typography, imagery and layout rules, allowing every format to feel consistent while remaining placement-specific.
Execution
Created a complete display ad system including animated banners, static creatives, responsive layouts and reusable design components for cross-platform deployment.
Impact
Delivered a scalable creative system that streamlined production, ensured brand consistency and enabled efficient adaptation across multiple campaign formats.

Royal Canin - Puppy Health Motion Ads

Challenge
Communicate puppy health benefits through simple, engaging creatives optimized for digital advertising placements.
Design Thinking
Focused on minimal layouts, bold messaging and motion-first storytelling to communicate key benefits quickly within limited ad attention spans.
Execution
Designed motion and static creatives across multiple display formats while maintaining a consistent visual language throughout the campaign.
Impact
Produced a flexible advertising toolkit that strengthened message consistency and adapted effectively across digital media placements.

Range Rover - Omnichannel Campaign Framework

Challenge
Create a unified campaign experience across multiple customer touchpoints while preserving Range Rover’s premium luxury positioning.
Design Thinking
Established a single visual system that could extend naturally across landing pages, CRM, social media and digital advertising without losing brand consistency.
Execution
Designed an integrated campaign ecosystem including landing pages, CRM templates, social creatives and digital advertising assets.
Impact
Delivered a cohesive omnichannel campaign framework that maintained a premium customer experience across every digital touchpoint.
